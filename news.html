Job Vacancy: Social Media Manager



Lutput Securities is seeking a talented and experienced Social Media Account Manager to join our team. If you have a passion for social media, a creative flair, and a proven track record in managing social media accounts, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute social media strategies to increase brand awareness and engagement.
  • Create and curate engaging content for various social media platforms, including but not limited to Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Instagram.
  • Monitor and respond to comments and messages on social media in a timely and professional manner.
  • Analyze social media data and metrics to track the success of campaigns and make data-driven decisions for improvement.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and emerging social media platforms.
  • Collaborate with marketing and design teams to create visually appealing and cohesive content.

Qualifications:

  • Proven experience managing social media accounts for a company or brand.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong knowledge of social media platforms, analytics, and scheduling tools.
  • Creative thinking and the ability to generate fresh ideas for content.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Attention to detail and strong organizational skills.

How to Apply:

If you are passionate about social media and meet the qualifications listed above, we encourage you to apply. Please submit your CV and a brief cover letter detailing your relevant experience to jobs@lutputsecurities.com. Be sure to include "Social Media Manager Application" in the subject line of your email.

Application Deadline: Until we find someone, so apply while you can!

Lutput Securities is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome and encourage candidates of all backgrounds to apply.


How to Create an Account with Lutput Securities


Step 1: Access the Registration Page

Begin by going to Registration page from our website.

Step 2: Fill in Your Information

You will be directed to the registration page. Here, you'll need to provide the following information:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Username
  • Email Address
  • Password
  • Confirm Password
  • Date of Birth
  • Phone Number
  • Address
  • Preferred Investment Type (e.g., Stocks, Bonds, Mutual Funds, Real Estate)
  • Subscribe to Newsletter (optional)

Step 3: Submit Your Information

After completing all the required fields, double-check your information for accuracy. Make sure your password is secure and follow any password requirements provided by Lutput Securities.

Once you're confident that your information is correct, click the "Create Account" or "Register" button at the bottom of the registration form.

Step 4: Verify Your Email

After submitting the registration form, you will likely receive an email from Lutput Securities to verify your email address. Open the email and follow the provided instructions to verify your email.

Step 5: Log In to Your New Account

Once your email is verified, return to the Lutput Securities website. Use the username and password you provided during registration to log in to your new account.

Step 6: Complete Your Profile

After logging in, you may be prompted to complete your profile by providing additional information, such as your financial goals and investment preferences. Fill in these details as needed.

Step 7: Start Investing

With your account created and profile completed, you are now ready to explore investment opportunities and manage your portfolio with Lutput Securities.
